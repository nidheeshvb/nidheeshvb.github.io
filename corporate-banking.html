Al-Khair long term deposit

With your authorization, we will strategically invest the deposit amount at an expected and mutually agreed-upon profit rate, ensuring a lasting and rewarding financial partnership.

Foreign Currencies investment

This investment deposit is available in US Dollars, Pounds Sterling and European Euro.

Continuous investment

The deposit is invested based on "Mudaraba" principle and renewed annually. It is available only in Kuwaiti Dinars, and the profits are distributed at the end of each financial year. 

Learn more

Al Dima

Al Dima" deposit in Kuwaiti Dinars is the best choice for fast fruitful investment. It is invested based on "Mudaraba" principle starting from one month up to 12 months and enabling you to earn monthly or quarterly returns without having to wait until the end of maturity date.

Learn more

Al Nuwwair

An innovative investment deposit, based on sharia principle "Wakala" Investment that promises you expected investment returns with the ability to reinvest the profits to achieve higher returns.

Learn more

Al Khomasiyah

Five years investment in Kuwaiti Dinars, with high profit rates to be deposited in the customer’s bank account on yearly basis. KFH manages this deposit according to “Mudarabah” Islamic principle.

Learn more

Al Kawther

"Al Kawther" deposit is an annual investment deposit in Kuwaiti Dinars, yet returns are monthly distributed without the need to wait until the end of year. This deposit is invested based on "Mudaraba" principle.

Learn more

Al Sidra

With its easy terms and low opening limit, every client can open "Al Sidra" investment deposit in Kuwaiti Dinars and enjoy its high rate profits that are distributed annually.
